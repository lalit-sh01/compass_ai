## Theme System

Compass.ai uses a **4-theme system** (2 light + 2 dark) that allows users to personalize their experience:

### Light Themes (Daytime)
- **Serene**: Soft blues & warm neutrals - clear, peaceful, trustworthy
- **Warm**: Soft peach & cool grays - nurturing, approachable, human

### Dark Themes (Nighttime)
- **Twilight**: Deep navy & soft cyan - calm, easy on eyes
- **Dusk**: Warm charcoal & amber - cozy, warm, inviting

**Key Principle**: Core brand elements (typography, spacing, layout, components) remain consistent across all themes. Only colors change.

---

## Core Brand Elements

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: DM Sans (rounded, friendly)
- **Secondary Font**: Inter (clean, readable)

### Color Philosophy
- **Light Themes**: Soft, calming backgrounds with purposeful accents
- **Dark Themes**: Easy on eyes, reduced contrast, gentle glows

### Tone of Voice
- Supportive friend • Calm • Empathetic • Intentional
- Conversational and warm, never corporate or cold

---

## Key Design Principles

1. **Calm Over Chaos**: Every design decision should reduce cognitive load
2. **Breathing Room**: Generous spacing creates peace and clarity
3. **Intentional Color**: Use accent colors sparingly for maximum impact
4. **Soft Transitions**: Smooth, gentle animations - never jarring
5. **Human-Centered**: Design for wellbeing alongside achievement
