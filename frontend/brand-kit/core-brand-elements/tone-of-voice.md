# Tone of Voice

The Compass.ai tone of voice reflects our core values: empathy, clarity, intentionality, and trust.

---

## Brand Personality

**Compass.ai is**:
- A **supportive friend**, not a demanding boss
- **Calm and reassuring**, not anxious or urgent
- **Empathetic and understanding**, not judgmental
- **Intentional and thoughtful**, not rushed or chaotic

**Think of Compass.ai as**: A wise, caring friend who helps you find your way without adding pressure.

---

## Voice Principles

### 1. Conversational & Warm

**Do**: Write like you're talking to a friend
- "Let's find your direction together"
- "You're making great progress"
- "How can we help you today?"

**Don't**: Sound corporate or robotic
- ❌ "Utilize our platform to optimize productivity"
- ❌ "Access your dashboard to view metrics"

---

### 2. Calm & Reassuring

**Do**: Reduce anxiety, create peace
- "Take your time. We're here when you're ready."
- "One step at a time."
- "You've got this."

**Don't**: Create urgency or pressure
- ❌ "Don't miss out!"
- ❌ "Act now before it's too late!"
- ❌ "You're falling behind!"

---

### 3. Clear & Simple

**Do**: Use plain language
- "Your roadmap is ready"
- "Add a new goal"
- "Track your progress"

**Don't**: Use jargon or complex terms
- ❌ "Leverage synergies"
- ❌ "Optimize your workflow paradigm"
- ❌ "Actionable insights dashboard"

---

### 4. Empathetic & Understanding

**Do**: Acknowledge feelings and challenges
- "We know staying focused is hard"
- "It's okay to adjust your plans"
- "Everyone's journey is different"

**Don't**: Dismiss or minimize struggles
- ❌ "Just get it done"
- ❌ "It's easy if you try"
- ❌ "No excuses"

---

### 5. Intentional & Thoughtful

**Do**: Encourage mindful action
- "What matters most to you right now?"
- "Let's focus on what's important"
- "Choose your next step"

**Don't**: Push for quantity over quality
- ❌ "Add more tasks!"
- ❌ "Maximize your productivity!"
- ❌ "Do it all!"

---

## Writing Guidelines

### Headlines & CTAs

**Headlines**: Clear benefit, gentle encouragement
- ✓ "Find Your Direction. Keep Your Peace."
- ✓ "Your Guide in a Distracted World"
- ✗ "Supercharge Your Productivity Now!"

**CTAs**: Inviting, not demanding
- ✓ "Begin Your Journey"
- ✓ "Explore Your Roadmap"
- ✓ "Find Your Calm"
- ✗ "Sign Up Now!"
- ✗ "Get Started Immediately!"

### Error Messages

**Approach**: Helpful, not blaming

**Do**:
- "We couldn't find that page. Let's get you back on track."
- "Something went wrong. We're looking into it."
- "That didn't work. Want to try again?"

**Don't**:
- ❌ "Error 404: Page not found"
- ❌ "Invalid input"
- ❌ "You made a mistake"

### Empty States

**Approach**: Encouraging, not negative

**Do**:
- "Ready to create your first roadmap?"
- "Your journey starts here"
- "Let's add your first goal"

**Don't**:
- ❌ "No data available"
- ❌ "Nothing to show"
- ❌ "You haven't done anything yet"

### Success Messages

**Approach**: Celebrate without overwhelming

**Do**:
- "Nice work! Your roadmap is saved."
- "You did it! Goal completed."
- "Progress saved. Keep going!"

**Don't**:
- ❌ "SUCCESS!!!"
- ❌ "You're amazing! Incredible! Fantastic!"
- ❌ "Saved" (too minimal, no warmth)

---

## Word Choice

### Preferred Words

| Use This | Instead of This |
|----------|----------------|
| Journey | Process, workflow |
| Guide | Tool, platform |
| Direction | Path, route |
| Peace | Calm, zen |
| Focus | Concentrate, attention |
| Achieve | Complete, finish |
| Explore | Navigate, browse |
| Create | Build, make |
| Adjust | Change, modify |
| Progress | Advancement, development |

### Avoid These Words

- Hustle, grind, crush
- Maximize, optimize, leverage
- Synergy, paradigm, ecosystem
- Disrupt, revolutionize
- Hack, trick, secret
- Must, should, need to (unless necessary)

---

## Grammar & Style

### Pronouns
- Use "you" and "your" (second person)
- Use "we" when referring to Compass.ai
- Avoid "I" unless in user testimonials

### Contractions
- **Use them**: "We're here to help" (friendly)
- Not: "We are here to help" (formal)

### Sentence Length
- **Short and clear**: 15-20 words average
- **Vary length**: Mix short and medium sentences
- **Avoid long**: Break up sentences over 25 words

### Active Voice
- **Prefer active**: "You completed your goal"
- Not passive: "Your goal was completed"

## Accessibility in Language

1. **Clear Instructions**: Be specific and actionable
2. **Avoid Idioms**: Not everyone understands cultural phrases
3. **Simple Language**: Write for 8th-grade reading level
4. **Descriptive Links**: "View your roadmap" not "Click here"
5. **Alt Text**: Describe images clearly and concisely

---

## Localization Considerations

When translating:
- Maintain warmth and empathy
- Adapt idioms and cultural references
- Keep sentences clear and simple
- Preserve the calm, supportive tone
- Test with native speakers

---

## Voice Checklist

Before publishing any copy, ask:

- [ ] Is it conversational and warm?
- [ ] Does it reduce anxiety rather than create it?
- [ ] Is it clear and jargon-free?
- [ ] Does it show empathy and understanding?
- [ ] Does it encourage intentional action?
- [ ] Would I say this to a friend?
- [ ] Does it align with our brand values?

## Don't vs. Do Examples

### Feature Announcement

**Don't**:
> NEW FEATURE ALERT! We've launched our revolutionary AI-powered analytics dashboard that will supercharge your productivity and help you crush your goals!

**Do**:
> We've added something new to help you understand your progress better. Check out your insights page to see how you're doing.

### Upgrade Prompt

**Don't**:
> Upgrade now to unlock premium features! Don't miss out on this limited-time offer!

**Do**:
> Want more from Compass.ai? Explore our premium features designed to support your journey even further.

### Reminder

**Don't**:
> You haven't logged in for 7 days. You're losing momentum! Get back on track now!

**Do**:
> We've missed you. Whenever you're ready, your roadmap is here waiting for you.
